= Lock your customer by offering subscription program =
Empower your customers to keep repurchase and keeping them consistently engaged in long-term relationships. At the end of the period, you realize that your profit per customer has increase twice the amount compared to before.

= Increase customer retention with a customized loyalty program =
Customers get points every time they do transactions. The higher the transaction value, the more point they get. With each successful transaction, the more your members engage with your brand. A loyalty program gives you an easy way to encourage and motivate customer engagement and boosts retention.

= What does this plugin do? =
To use this plugin you need to register to our service at [https://usetada.com](https://usetada.com)

This plugin allow you to display a floating button in your website and embed your TADA membership page to allow your customer to check their membership without moving from your website.

This plugin also integrate WooCommerce with our API service by sending your WooCommerce order informations (customer phone number, order amount and order items) to our API when order are marked as completed.

== Installation ==
1. Install `TADA (Membership, Loyalty, Referral & Subscription Platform)` plugin from your WordPress dashboard or Upload "usetada" folder to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress.
1. Go to `TADA` settings and Active `TADA` plugin
